Quack-a-Tab - Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy describes how Quack-a-Tab ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles information when you use our Chrome extension.

Data Collection

Quack-a-Tab does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All the information used by the extension is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API and is never sent to external servers.

Information Usage

  • The extension stores tab names and your recently used custom names locally on your device.
  • This information is used solely to provide the tab renaming functionality.
  • No analytics, tracking, or fingerprinting technologies are used.
  • We do not have access to any of the data stored by the extension.

Required Permissions

Quack-a-Tab requires the following permissions:

  • tabs: To access and modify the titles of browser tabs.
  • storage: To save your custom tab names and recent names locally.

Data Storage and Retention

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API.
  • Tab name data is automatically deleted when you close a tab.
  • Recent names history persists until you uninstall the extension.
  • No data is retained on any external servers.

Third-Party Services

Quack-a-Tab does not use any third-party services or libraries that would collect or process user data.

Children's Privacy

The extension does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If there are material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you through an extension update. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please create an issue in the repository.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date indicated at the top of this page.
