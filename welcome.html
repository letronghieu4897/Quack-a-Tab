Welcome to Tab Renamer

Organize your browser tabs with custom names for better productivity and workflow

Key Features

Custom Tab Names

Rename any browser tab to something more memorable or descriptive. Never get lost in a sea of similar tab titles again!

Name History

Quickly reuse previously created names from your history, making it easier to maintain consistency across similar tabs.

Auto Restore

Your custom names persist even when pages refresh or update their own titles, ensuring your organization stays intact.

Dark Mode

Switch between light and dark themes to match your browser theme or preference for night-time browsing.

How It Works

1

Click the Extension Icon

Find the Tab Renamer icon in your browser's extension toolbar and click it to open the popup interface.

2

Enter a New Name

Type your desired tab name in the input field. Try to use short, descriptive names for better organization.

3

Save Your Changes

Click the "Save" button or press Enter to apply the new name to your current tab. The tab title will instantly update.

4

Reset When Needed

If you want to go back to the original tab name, simply click the "Reset" button to restore the default title.

Pro Tips

Use Short Prefixes

Add short prefixes like "[WIP]", "[REF]", or "[TODO]" to quickly identify tab purposes at a glance.

Keyboard Shortcut

After clicking the extension icon, just type your new name and press Enter - no need to click the Save button!

Team Collaboration

When working with a team, use consistent naming conventions for shared projects or research to stay organized.
